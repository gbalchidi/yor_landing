Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2025 | Effective Date: March 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to YOR ("App", "Service", "we", "us", or "our"). YOR is a social mood-sharing application that allows users to share their current mood with friends, react to friends' moods, and add short text statuses.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using YOR, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

YOR is operated by Pulse Circle L.L.C-FZ, a company registered in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (Free Zone).

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@yor.space.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and profile picture (if provided).

Mood Data: When you share your mood, we collect the mood type you select (e.g., "slay", "normi", "kucheryavo") and the timestamp of sharing.

Status Text: If you choose to add a text status to your mood, we collect the status content (up to 60 characters).

Social Connections: When you add friends via invitation links, we collect your friend list, including who invited you and the time of connection.

Reactions: When you react to a friend's mood, we collect the reaction type, the target friend, and the trigger method (long press or double tap).

Communications: If you contact our support team, we collect the content of your messages.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information: We collect device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including session duration, features used, mood sharing frequency, streak data, and in-app actions.

Push Notification Tokens: If you enable push notifications, we collect your device's push token to deliver notifications.

Log Data: Our servers automatically record information including your IP address, access times, and app version.

Attribution Data: We may collect UTM parameters (source, medium, campaign, term, ad) from the link that brought you to the App, for the purpose of understanding how users discover YOR.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise geolocation data.
  • We do not access your phone's contacts, camera, microphone, or photo library (unless you explicitly grant permission for a profile picture).
  • We do not collect financial or payment information.
  • We do not use cookies or third-party tracking pixels within the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service: Display your mood and status to your friends, deliver reactions, maintain your friend list, and calculate streaks.
  • Send Push Notifications: Notify you when a friend reacts to your mood, remind you to share your mood, and send other relevant updates.
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns and analytics events (in aggregate) to improve features, fix bugs, and develop new functionality.
  • Ensure Safety: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Use.
  • Communicate with You: Respond to support requests and send service-related announcements.

We do not use your mood data, statuses, or reactions to serve advertising. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Sensitive Data — Mood and Emotional Information

YOR collects data about your emotional state (mood selections, statuses, streaks). We recognize that this data may be considered sensitive under certain privacy regulations.

Our commitments regarding mood data:

  • Mood data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the App (sharing moods with your friends and receiving reactions).
  • We do not use mood data to build psychological profiles, target advertising, or share with data brokers.
  • We do not share mood data with employers, insurers, educational institutions, or any entity that could use it to make decisions about you.
  • Mood data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the Service (see Section 8).

5. How We Share Your Information

5.1 With Your Friends

Your mood, status text, and profile information are visible to users on your friend list. Reactions you send are visible to the recipient. This sharing is the core purpose of YOR.

5.2 With Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App, including:

  • Cloud Hosting (Google Cloud)
  • Analytics (anonymized/pseudonymized)
  • Push Notification Services (APNs and FCM)

All service providers are bound by contractual obligations to protect your data.

5.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

5.4 Business Transfers

If YOR is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5.5 We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal information.
  • Share your data with advertisers.
  • Share your data with data brokers.

  • Correct inaccurate data.

7. Children's Privacy

7.1 Age Restriction

YOR is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7.2 United States — COPPA Compliance

In compliance with COPPA, we do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under 13 in the United States.

7.3 United Arab Emirates — Digital Safety Law

In compliance with UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 3 of 2022, we implement age verification at registration. Users under 13 are not permitted to create accounts.

8. Data Retention

Account Data: Retained for as long as your account is active. Upon deletion, data deleted/anonymized within 30 days.

Mood Data: Current mood active until replaced. Historical mood data retained in anonymized/aggregated form.

Status Text: Deleted when the associated mood is replaced.

Reactions: Expire after 24 hours (TTL). Metadata may be retained in anonymized form.

Push Notification Data: Retained while account is active and notifications enabled.

Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.

9. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Access controls
  • Regular security assessments

9.1 Data Breach Notification

10. International Data Transfers

YOR is operated from the UAE. Data may be processed in jurisdictions outside your country. We implement:
