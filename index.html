Features

built for real connection

pick your mood in a swipe

no typing, no photos. just swipe through moods like chilling, vibing, slaying, cooked — and share with friends instantly.

see how your people are feeling

your feed shows friends' moods in real time. know when someone's having a great day or needs support — without them having to post about it.

social without performance pressure

no likes, no followers, no content to create. react to a friend's mood with one tap and let them know you're there. YOR is about real connection, not performance.

How It Works

three steps. that's it.

1

pick your mood

swipe left or right to choose how you're feeling right now. add a short status for context if you want.

2

friends see your vibe

your mood appears on your friends' feed. only your friends can see it — no public profiles, no strangers.

3

react and stay connected

friends react to your mood. you react to theirs. build streaks by sharing daily and keep the connection alive.
